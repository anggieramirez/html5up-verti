About Us

Strke-A-Lott is a Bowling Pro Shop that is owned and operated by Gary Robinson and Professional Bowler Wes Malott. Gary and Wes are in the Pro Shop business because of their love for the sport of Bowling and they will to extend their 20+ year experience and knowlegde from the industry to others.

Strike-A-Lott has two locations to service all your bowling needs in the Austin area. Strike-A-Lott North is located inside Highland Lanes and Strike-A-Lott South is located inside Westgate Lanes. At our locations you will also find the LARGEST inventory in Central Texas!

Strike-A-Lott strives to give you the best customer service possible. This includes going out on the lanes and watching the customer bowl to receive all the information needed to match up to the customer with the best bowling ball to get the best angles and the most strikes.
