Wes Malott

Wes is a 10-time National Title holder on the PBA Tour including the 2013 U.S. Open. Wes also has 17 PBA Regional titles and has been a member of the PBA since 2001. Wes has been married to his wife Maryiedth for 15 years and they have 3 children, Jordan (13), Camden (9) and Brooke (6). When you are not watching Wes bowl on ESPN, you will typically find him working at Strike-A-Lott North, if he is not at the baseball field with one of his 3 children.
